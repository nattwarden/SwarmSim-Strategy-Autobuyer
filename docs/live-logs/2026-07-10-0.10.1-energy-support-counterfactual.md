## Energy Support Broker notes (version-scoped)

For observed 0.10.1 state, Energy lane mostly acted as a support lane.

- Lepidoptera: good safe background lever; larger chunk gave better gain per run than tiny +5 style behavior.
- Clone Larvae: strong when larva pipeline can absorb it; gave clear immediate larva jump in this snapshot.
- House of Mirrors: in this snapshot window it spent reserve but did not improve territory/sec immediately.
- Wait: useful when preserving reserve for stronger cast timing.
